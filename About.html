About Me

I am a graduate student in the ECE Department of UW-Madison working with Prof. Yu Hen Hu.

I am also working on computer vision algorithms for camera arrays. I am trying to develop a better method of visualizing the input from an array of live cameras.

I have a BS in Electrical Engineering from UW-Madison where I focused on signal processing techniques. During my undergraduate work, I helped perform signal analysis on lake level time series to identify trends in lake levels in northern Wisconsin. I also helped in the development a sensor system for testing hamstring strength in UW-Madison athletes so that coaches and physical trainers could better know if athletes are fully recovered from hamstring injuries
